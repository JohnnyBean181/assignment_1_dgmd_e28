Introduction

Hello, my name is Yiming Xia, and I was born and currently live in Shanghai, China. Over the past few years, Shanghai has developed rapidly into a modern metropolitan city. You can feel its vibrancy through its well-developed transportation, diverse restaurants, and exciting entertainment options.
I graduated from Tongji University about a decade ago with a degree in Software Engineering. After graduation, I began working for an organization primarily focused on financial regulation. In addition to handling my daily responsibilities, I have made significant contributions to my employer by providing technical support and solutions.

Favorite Place

 
Shanghai Disneyland opened in 2016. Despite the presence of Tokyo Disneyland and Hong Kong Disneyland nearby, Shanghai Disneyland has attracted a large number of visitors from mainland China and has brought immense joy to them.
This photo was taken in 2017, just a year after its grand opening, when my wife and I dined at one of the park's restaurants and even had the chance to meet the beloved Donald Duck. In recent years, Shanghai Disneyland has continued to expand, adding two new themed areas: Toy Story Land and Zootopia. However, it remains consistently crowded, and ticket prices have steadily increased.
If someone were to ask me the best time to visit, I would definitely say the first day of the school term for elementary students, as parents are unlikely to bring their children to the park on that day.
